Horsens, Aarhus, Skagen, Aalborg- Denmark - September 2023



Touristic Attractions

We arrived on Tuesday evening, in Billund.

On the first day, after a long sleep, we visited the Prison Museum in Horsens.

On the second day, we went to Aarhus and walked in the city center.

We walked on the beach on the Baltic Sea, where we took some awesome pictures.

We also went north, to Skagen, where we visited a famous and very interesting landmark, Grennen, the meeting point of North Sea and Baltic Sea.

Even though it was September, the weather was not our friend, so I recommend you take some jackets with you if you plan on going in the north of Denmark.

We walked in the city center of Aalborg, which is a very beautiful city, and we also visied the Musical Park. In this park, every tree has a famous song attached to it and you can play it by just pressing a button.

We went to the Bothanical Park, where we also saw a buch of butterflies.

We spent our last days in Horsens, walking in the city center and malls, and also eating at nice restaurants.

Traditions & Food

Fish is a very common food in Denmark, given that it's a peninsula.

Another famous dish in Denmark is the hotdog, which you can find in every city center.

There is a famous chocolate milk in Denmark, Cocio, and I recommend you at least try it if you plan on visiting this country.

Dannish people are not the ones to spend a lot of time in the kitchen, so you'll find a lot of pre-prepared meals in the supermarkets, especially gratinated potatoes (which taste amazing).
