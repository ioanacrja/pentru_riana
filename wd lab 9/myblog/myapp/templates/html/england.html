London, England- August 2023



Touristic Attractions

We arrived at the airport on Friday night.

The next day, we visited The Canary Wharf, a beautiful avenue of London and we enjoyed the beautiful view from Sky Garden.

On this trip, we didn't really visit museums because we felt like we needed to see the beauty of London and we didn't have enough time to visit everything.

Everyday, we made 20-30k steps while seeing the most beautiful English streets.

Of course, we stopped by at the Big Ben, the London Eye, China Town and we enjoyed feeding squirrels and ducks in Hyde Park.

On our last days, we visited a small city near London, Corby.

Traditions & Food

The thing I saw only in London so far were the street artists: dancers, musicians and painters making art in the center of the capital.

In terms of food traditons, there are not a lot.

Of course, we tasted the classics: english breakfast, fish&chips and tea, but, honestly, they were not my favourites.
